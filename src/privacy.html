1. Information we collect

Website usage

Like most websites, our hosting and analytics providers may automatically collect technical information such as your browser type, device type, pages visited, and referring URL. This data is typically collected in aggregate and used to keep the site secure and running smoothly.

BRUSH app

When you use the app, we may collect information needed to operate the service — for example, account details, in-app activity, streaks, badges, and interactions with features such as AI Shadow. We will update this policy when data practices change.

What we do not collect: payment details, government IDs, or any financial account information. BRUSH is free to play. Strokes are a virtual play currency with no cash value.

2. How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

  • operate, maintain, and improve the website and app;
  • provide features such as leaderboards, streaks, and AI Shadow;
  • respond to support requests and enforce our terms;
  • comply with legal obligations and protect against abuse or fraud.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for pay-to-win mechanics or real-money gambling.

3. Legal bases (EEA & UK)

If you are in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, we process personal data on the following bases:

  • Contract — to provide the BRUSH service you request.
  • Legitimate interests — to secure our services, prevent abuse, and understand how the website and app are used, where those interests are not overridden by your rights.
  • Legal obligation — where we must retain or disclose information to comply with applicable law.

4. Sharing with service providers

We use trusted third-party services to run BRUSH. They process data only on our instructions and for the purposes described in this policy:

  • Hosting providers — to deliver the website (for example, GitHub Pages or similar static hosting).
  • App platform providers — such as Apple App Store and Google Play, which may process data according to their own privacy policies when you download or use the app.

We may also disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of BRUSH, our users, or others.

5. Data retention

We keep personal data only as long as needed to provide the service, fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or comply with legal obligations. We may retain limited records longer where required by law or for legitimate business needs such as dispute resolution.

6. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) on the website. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing.

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below. If you are in the EEA or UK, you may also lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

8. Children

BRUSH is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

9. International transfers

Improvs and our service providers may process data in countries other than where you live, including the United States. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses or equivalent mechanisms.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Material changes may also be communicated through the app or on this website.
